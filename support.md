## Common questions

**Where is my data stored?**
On your device. Thaler is local-first — your accounts and transactions are not
held on our servers. See our [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.md).

**How do I back up?**
Menu → Google Drive Sync. Backups go to _your own_ Google Drive.

**How do I delete all my data?**
Menu → **Delete all data**. This erases all local data immediately and cannot be
undone. To remove cloud backups, delete them from your Google Drive and revoke
the app's access in your Google Account settings.

**How do I request data deletion?**
Use **Delete all data** in the app for on-device data. For anything else (e.g.
previously collected anonymous diagnostics, if you ever opted in), email us at
the address above and we'll handle it.
