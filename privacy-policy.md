# Thaler — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 26 June 2026**

Thaler ("the app", "we", "us") is a personal-finance app that helps you track
spending, budgets, and net worth. This policy explains what data the app
handles, where it goes, and the choices you have.

**Our core principle: your financial data stays on your device.** Thaler is
local-first. The accounts, transactions, budgets, assets, and debts you enter
are stored in a database **on your phone**, not on our servers. We do not have a
server that holds your financial data, and we never sell your data.

---

## 1. What data the app handles, and where it stays

**a) Financial data you enter (accounts, transactions, budgets, assets, debts,
notes, attachments).**
Stored **only on your device**. We cannot see it. It leaves your device only if
_you_ choose to back up to your own Google Drive or export a file (see below).

**b) Crash reports & usage analytics — opt-in, OFF by default.**
If (and only if) you turn on "Share diagnostics & analytics" in Settings, the
app sends **anonymous** crash reports and basic usage events (e.g. which screens
are opened) to Google Firebase (Crashlytics & Analytics) to help us fix bugs and
improve the app. This **never** includes your transactions, amounts, account
names, or other financial details. You can turn it off at any time.

**c) Bank/wallet notification access — opt-in.**
If you enable auto-capture, Thaler reads incoming bank/payment **notifications**
on your device to pre-fill draft transactions for your review. This processing
happens **on your device**; the notification content is not transmitted to us.
You can disable this any time in your device settings or in the app.

**d) Microphone (voice add) — only while you use it.**
If you use "Voice add", your speech is sent to your device's built-in
speech-recognition service (e.g. Google) to convert it to text, the same way
voice typing works. We do not record or store your audio.

**e) Google Drive / Google Sheets backup & sync — optional, you initiate it.**
If you connect your Google account, backups and sheet syncs are written to
**your own** Google Drive/Sheets. The data goes to your Google account, not to
us. We request the narrowest scope needed to create and manage these files.

**f) Biometric unlock (fingerprint/face) — on-device only.**
Handled entirely by your device's secure hardware. We never receive your
biometric data.

**g) Camera / photos (receipt scanning) — only when you scan.**
Used to capture or pick a receipt image for on-device text extraction. Images
stay on your device unless you attach and back them up yourself.

---

## 2. What we do NOT do

- We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your data.
- We do **not** collect your financial data on our servers.
- We do **not** show third-party ads.
- We do **not** track you across other apps or websites.

---

## 3. Permissions and why

| Permission           | Why                                                                            |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Notification access  | Optional auto-capture of bank/payment notifications, on-device                 |
| Microphone           | Optional voice entry of transactions                                           |
| Camera / photos      | Optional receipt scanning                                                      |
| Notifications (post) | Budget alerts, bill reminders, recaps                                          |
| Biometric            | Optional app lock                                                              |
| Internet             | Live exchange/price lookups, and (only if enabled) Google backup + diagnostics |

You can decline or revoke any of these in your device settings; the app remains
usable, with the related feature turned off.

---

## 4. Data retention & deletion

- Your data lives on your device until you delete it.
- **Delete everything in the app:** Menu → **Delete all data** erases all local
  data immediately and irreversibly.
- **Uninstalling** the app removes its on-device data.
- **Cloud backups** you created live in your own Google Drive; delete them from
  Google Drive, and revoke the app's access in your Google Account settings.
- **Diagnostics:** turning off "Share diagnostics & analytics" stops further
  collection. To request deletion of previously collected anonymous diagnostics,
  contact us at the email below.

---

## 5. Children

Thaler is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your
country) and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

---

## 6. Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. GDPR in the EU/UK, CCPA in California), you may
have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data, and to withdraw
consent. Because your financial data is stored on your own device, you already
control it directly (view, edit, export, or delete in-app). For anything else,
contact us.
